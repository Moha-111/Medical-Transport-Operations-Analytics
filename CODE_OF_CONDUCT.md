# Code of Conduct — Wasl Platform

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, welcoming, and professional environment, we as contributors and maintainers of **Wasl** pledge to make participation in our project a respectful experience for everyone — regardless of age, background, nationality, religion, gender, experience level, or professional role.

We are building technology for healthcare. Our conduct must reflect the seriousness and respect that healthcare professionals demonstrate every day.

---

## Our Standards

### Encouraged Behaviors

- **Professional communication** — treat every interaction as a business conversation
- **Constructive feedback** — critique code and ideas, never people
- **Cultural respect** — this is a Saudi-first platform; respect Islamic values and Arabic culture
- **Patience** — contributors come from different backgrounds and experience levels
- **Transparency** — be honest about limitations, bugs, and uncertainties
- **Privacy-first mindset** — always protect patient data and operational security

### Unacceptable Behaviors

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Sharing or requesting real patient data
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Disrespecting religious or cultural norms
- Trolling, inflammatory comments, or deliberate provocation

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces:
- GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, and Discussions
- Code reviews and commit messages
- Direct communications related to the project
- Public representations of the project (conferences, social media)
